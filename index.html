Winter Hot Chocolate Recipe

Warm up this winter with a cozy cup of hot chocolate!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows for topping

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat.
  2. Stir in cocoa powder and sugar until dissolved.
  3. Continue heating until the mixture is hot but not boiling.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows.

Enjoy your warm cup of hot chocolate and stay cozy!
